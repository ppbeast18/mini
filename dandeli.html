Overview

Experience the ultimate getaway to Gokarna and Dandeli, where you’ll discover breathtaking beaches, ancient temples, and thrilling adventures. Gokarna offers pristine stretches of sand like Belekan beach,Paradise beach and Half Moon beach, OM beach, along with the revered Murudeshwar Temple. Enjoy water Waterfalls and scenic coastal treks. In Dandeli, explore the diverse Nature, brave the rapids with river rafting on the Kali River. Indulge in kayaking and canoeing amidst picturesque landscapes. Unwind, find spirituality, and seek adventure on this remarkable weekend trip.
