Overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the picturesque landscapes of Darjeeling and Gangtok. Witness breathtaking sunrises over the Himalayas , explore vibrant monasteries, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of the region. Discover Darjeeling’s iconic tea gardens, stroll along the Mall Road, and delve into the spiritual ambiance of Gangtok’s monastic sites. From the tranquil beauty of Tsomgo Lake to the bustling markets of Gangtok, every moment of your trip promises to be a captivating adventure. Join us for a remarkable exploration of two Himalayan gems, where nature’s grandeur meets cultural allure.

Itinerary

Day 1             Arrival in Bagdogra - Transfer to Darjeeling

 Arrive at Bagdogra Airport or New Jalpaiguri Railway Station.

Day 2             Darjeeling Sightseeing

   Wake up early to witness the sunrise over the Himalayas from Tiger Hill. Marvel at the breathtaking views of Mt. Kanchenjunga and the surrounding peaks.

   Visit Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop on your way back to Darjeeling.

   After breakfast, visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park, and Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre.

   Explore the vibrant markets of Darjeeling and indulge in some shopping for local handicrafts and tea

Day 3             Transfer to Gangtok

   Check out from your hotel after breakfast and proceed to Gangtok (approximately 3-4 hours).

   Enroute, stop by the picturesque Teesta River and take in the scenic beauty.

   Continue your journey to Gangtok and check-in at your hotel.

Day 4          Gangtok Sightseeing

    Visit the iconic Rumtek Monastery, one of the largest and most significant monasteries in Sikkim.

    Explore the enchanting Enchey Monastery and admire its stunning architecture and religious significance.

    Visit the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology to learn about Tibetan culture, history, and art

   Explore the serene beauty of Banjhakri Falls and Energy Park, a popular tourist spot surrounded by lush greenery.

Day 5          Excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir

   Continue your journey to Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a sacred shrine dedicated to an Indian Army soldier.

   Enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding landscapes and spend some time by the serene lake

   Rest of the day at leisure for shopping or exploring the local cuisine.

Day 6             Departure from Gangtok

   Enjoy a delicious breakfast at the hotel.

   Check out from the hotel and transfer to Bagdogra Airport or New Jalpaiguri Railway Station for your onward journey.

    Bid farewell to the beautiful hills of Darjeeling and Gangtok as you depart with cherished memories of your trip.
